The Name
Where the name comes from

Tigris takes its name from the Tigris River — one of the two rivers that cradled Mesopotamia, the birthplace of civilization. It was there, between the Tigris and the Euphrates, that humanity first built systems — language, law, agriculture, trade. The foundations that everything after was built on.

We chose that name because we believe AI is at a similar inflection point. Not AI as a novelty or a feature — AI as infrastructure. The kind that reshapes entire industries from the ground up. Tigris Tech Labs exists to build that foundation.

What We Do
AI-native products for industries that demand precision

Tigris Tech Labs is an AI-native product company. We don't consult. We don't build generic tools. We build software products — each one purpose-built for a specific industry, trained on its language, and designed for the professionals who actually work in it.

Every product we ship has intelligence at its core, not bolted on as an afterthought. We go deep into industries that others skim — because surface-level AI doesn't survive contact with real-world complexity.

Our Products
Purpose-built intelligence
Peptide Cortex
Peptide Protocol Intelligence

Cortex brings AI into peptide research and protocol design. It reads the literature, understands mechanisms of action, and helps researchers build protocols faster — without cutting corners on science. From dosing frameworks to interaction analysis, Cortex turns hours of manual research into minutes of guided intelligence.

And more to come. We're actively exploring new verticals where domain expertise is fragmented, decision-making is high-stakes, and AI can do more than automate — it can elevate. Every new product follows the same principle: go deep, earn trust, build precision.

How We Build
Our principles

Domain First

We immerse ourselves in the industry before writing a single line of code. We talk to practitioners, read the research, and learn the pain points firsthand.

Precision Over Features

We'd rather do three things perfectly than thirty things adequately. Every feature ships when it's ready to be trusted by professionals whose livelihoods depend on accuracy.

Intelligence, Not Automation

Our products don't replace experts. They make experts more expert. We build tools that enhance human judgment, not tools that bypass it.

The best AI products don't feel like AI products. They feel like the tool you always wished existed — one that speaks your language, understands your workflow, and makes you better at what you already do.

That's what we're building at Tigris Tech Labs. One industry at a time.
