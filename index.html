About
We don't build tools.
We build intelligence.

Tigris Tech Labs is an AI-native product company. Every product we ship is built from the ground up with intelligence at its core — not bolted on as a feature. We go deep into industries that others skim — where precision isn't a nice-to-have, it's the difference between a product that works and one that gets ignored.

The name comes from the Tigris River — one of the two rivers that cradled Mesopotamia, the birthplace of civilization, where humanity first built the systems that everything after was built on. AI is at a similar inflection. Tigris exists to build that foundation, one industry at a time.

The best AI products don't feel like AI products. They feel like the tool you always wished existed.

Philosophy
How we build.
Why it matters.
01
Domain First
We learn the industry before we write a line of code. Every product starts with months of domain immersion — talking to practitioners, reading the literature, understanding the pain.
02
Precision Over Features
We'd rather do three things perfectly than thirty things adequately. Every feature ships when it's ready to be trusted by professionals whose livelihoods depend on accuracy.
03
Intelligence, Not Automation
Our products don't replace thinking — they enhance it. We build tools that make experts more expert, not tools that make experts unnecessary.
