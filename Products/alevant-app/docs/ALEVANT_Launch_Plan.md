# ALEVANT — Launch & Press Plan

**Goal:** turn Bichi's 90-day pilot results into a category-defining narrative in the real-estate trade press, then ride the wave into multi-tenant GA.

---

## Press window — Day 90 of Bichi pilot

**Trigger metrics** (any one ships the launch):
- 2x production vs. Bichi's prior 90-day run
- ≥ 3 listings closed via ALEVANT-sourced leads
- Sofia handling ≥ 100 conversations/week with ≥ 25% qualifying
- 1+ "wow moment" — a press-worthy story (Sofia booking a $4M cash buyer at 2am Miami time, etc.)

**Don't ship the launch without a wow moment.** Number-only stories don't get picked up. We need the agent's quote, the buyer's quote, and a specific dollar amount.

## Launch sequence

**Week -2 (D-14):**
- Reach out to Inman + The Real Deal with embargoed pitch + fact sheet + Bichi available for interview.
- Both outlets get exclusive interview slots.

**Week -1 (D-7):**
- Tier 2 outlets receive embargoed pitch.
- Tier 3 outlets receive non-embargoed pitch with a "talk after Inman publishes" note.

**Day 0:**
- Inman publishes 6am ET.
- The Real Deal follows by 9am ET.
- ALEVANT social channels (IG, X, LinkedIn) post simultaneously.

**Days 1-7:**
- Tier 2 outlets follow up with their angles.

**Day 30 post-launch:**
- Recap post on company blog with metrics.
- Open enrollment for second pilot wave (5-10 agents).
- If financing event imminent, line up TechCrunch / The Information.

## Waitlist conversion plan

Pre-launch waitlist target: 200-500 agents.
Post-launch: track conversion to demo-requested → trial-started → paid.

**Funnel targets:**
- Waitlist → demo-requested: 25%
- Demo-requested → trial-started: 60%
- Trial-started → paid (post-90-day pilot): 30%

If 500 waitlist + 25% × 60% × 30% = 22 paid in first 6 months. At Agent tier ($399/mo) = $8,778 MRR. At Team tier mix: closer to $20-30k MRR.

## Risk perimeter — don't ship the launch if any of these are true

- Sofia has had a Fair Housing incident in the prior 30 days.
- Sofia has had a TCPA complaint.
- HeyGen avatar misrepresented Bichi in a way that drew criticism.
- Bichi's brokerage (KW Capital Realty) hasn't given written sign-off.
- A real-estate consumer-protection class action is active in Florida around AI bots.

**The launch story is "AI changes real estate."** A single compliance incident becomes "AI breaks real estate." We protect the launch by protecting the perimeter.
