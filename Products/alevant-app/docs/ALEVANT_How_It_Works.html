03

A Day in the Life.

Tuesday in Miami. Bichi has three appointments, two new listings, and an investor closing on Friday. Here is what ALEVANT runs in the background while he runs his calendar.

3:42 AM
Inbound: international buyer
Sofia · IG DM
A Brazilian buyer DMs about a Brickell condo at 3:42 AM Miami time. Sofia answers in <8 seconds. Qualifies in Portuguese (V2 — for now in English with a translation note). Books Saturday showing. Logs to CRM. Bichi gets a single push at 7:00 AM with the summary, not 14 messages overnight.
7:00 AM
The Daily Standup
Cockpit · 90-second voice briefing
ALEVANT plays a 90-second AI briefing in Bichi's car: 3 hot inbounds overnight, 2 listings need price reviews (showing volume below threshold), 1 sphere right-call (Maria Delgado — 3-year close anniversary, building +22%), 4 deals at risk. One audio file, no scrolling.
9:15 AM
New listing taken
Vesper · 12-asset campaign
Bichi signs a $1.4M Coral Gables listing. He pastes the address into ALEVANT. Within 24 hours, Vesper delivers: cinematic film script + shot list, hero photography brief, custom microsite at 312coralway.bichi.miami, 8-page editorial brochure (PDF), 14-post 2-week social campaign (IG + X + TikTok + LinkedIn), email blast to 47 buyer-matches, just-listed event invite, and a press pitch for Mansion Global. Bichi reviews. Approves. Done.
11:30 AM
Showing presentation, live
Live Co-Pilot · earbud whisper
Bichi walks into a listing presentation against Compass and Douglas Elliman. Mid-meeting, the seller says, "Compass told me they'd list at $2.1M." Bichi's earbud quietly says: "Last 4 comparable closings within 0.4 miles, 90 days: $1.85M, $1.92M, $1.88M, $1.95M. Compass listing 2 blocks east currently 38 DOM, 1 price drop." Bichi answers with authority. Wins the listing.
2:00 PM
Investor underwriting
Underwriter · 30 seconds
A return investor texts Bichi: "1450 NW 7th, what do you think?" Bichi pastes the address into ALEVANT. 30 seconds: cap rate 6.8%, cash-on-cash 9.2%, BRRRR ARV $720k, STR projection $5.4k/mo (AirDNA), comp set, FIRPTA flag (foreign seller), 1031 timing if needed. Bichi sends the one-page underwrite to the investor. They schedule a showing.
4:30 PM
Sphere right-call
Sphere Brain · LinkedIn signal
ALEVANT detects a past client just announced a baby on LinkedIn. Sphere Brain surfaces: "Carlos and Andrea — closed Aug 2024 · 2BR Brickell condo · just announced baby · likely outgrowing space within 18 months · current building +14%." Suggested: handwritten card today, valuation update conversation in 90 days. Card text drafted. Bichi adds three words and signs.
6:00 PM
Handoff to Sofia
Sofia · solo
Bichi clocks out. Sofia takes inbound until 8:30am. Vesper queues tomorrow's 4 social posts for Bichi's morning approval. Transaction Brain nudges a slow lender on Friday's investor closing. The platform operates while he sleeps.

The Modules.

Eleven core modules ship in V1. Every one is AI-native — not "with an AI sidebar bolted on." The platform is designed around Claude, not retrofitted.

01
Lead Inbox
Multi-source ingestion: Gmail, IG / X / TikTok / LinkedIn DMs, web forms, phone, FB Lead Ads. AI-classified, AI-routed.
02
Sofia
Voice + text ISA. Twilio number. Retell + Claude. Strict TCPA. Inbound V1, consented outbound V2.
03
Pipelines × 4
Buyer / Seller / Investor / Rental — each a distinct workflow. Rentals get speed-to-lead urgency; investors get underwriting.
04
Listings
Manual entry V1, MLS adapter ready. Vesper triggers automatically when a new listing is added.
05
Vesper Studio
Per-listing 12-asset campaign. Weekly content engine. Cinematic film, microsite, brochure, social, email, press.
06
Underwriter
Residential CMA + investor MF. Cap rate, cash-on-cash, BRRRR, STR via AirDNA. 30 seconds from address.
07
Pre-Con Tracker
Miami towers index. Deposit schedules. Developer reputation. Assignment opportunities. Delivery delays.
08
Transaction Brain
Contract-to-close orchestration. DocuSign-aware. Inspection / appraisal / loan / title / HOA / closing nudges.
09
Sphere Brain
LinkedIn / public records / social signal monitoring. Surfaces today's right call. Drafts the message.
10
Microsites
Auto-generated per premium listing. Magazine-tier. Multilingual-ready. Embedded Sofia chat.
11
Cockpit
Daily AI standup, signal feed, KPIs, weighted pipeline, deal-velocity scoring.
