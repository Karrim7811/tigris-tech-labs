Tenant Brand Layer

ALEVANT runs multi-tenant — every workspace owns a complete brand kit overlaid on the platform chrome. The pilot tenant is Bichi (Miami). Future tenants render with their own palette, typography, and voice. ALEVANT identity stays in the chrome corners; the agent's brand owns the canvas.
